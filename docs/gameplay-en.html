How to Play

Master the basics of EasyCTF and become a champion!

üéØ Game Objective

The goal is simple: capture the enemy team's flag and bring it back to your base! The team with the most captures when time runs out wins.

Victory Condition: The team with the highest score at the end of combat phase wins. In case of a tie, the winner is determined by the number of colored blocks placed.
Spectator Mode: Use /ctf spectator <game-name> to watch games without participating.
Spectator Features:
  • Enhanced scoreboard showing both teams' detailed status
  • Hotbar menu items (stats, player tracking, flag locations)
  • Important event notifications (flag captures, kill streaks, etc.)

üö© Flag System

Flag Appearance

Flags are beacons with team-colored glass on top, placed on a 3x3 iron block base.

How to Capture

Walk within 1.5 blocks of the enemy flag to pick it up. The flag will appear above your head.

Scoring

Return to your base with the enemy flag while your own flag is at base to score 1 point.

When Killed

If you die while carrying the flag, it drops at your death location. It returns automatically after 15 seconds.

Flag Carrier Restrictions:
  • Cannot use ender pearls
  • Cannot use elytra
  • Cannot ride vehicles
  • Has glowing effect (visible through walls)

üõ°Ô∏è Build Phase Strategy

Defense Building

Create obstacles around your flag using team-colored blocks (red/blue concrete and stained glass). These blocks must be connected to your beacon or existing team blocks. Blocks purchased from the shop (stone, wood, dirt, etc.) can be placed freely without connection restrictions.

Shop Usage

Right-click the emerald in your inventory near spawn to open the shop. Buy weapons, armor, and special items.

Team Coordination

Discuss strategies with your team. Decide who will attack and who will defend before combat begins.

üîó Team Block Connection System

Basic Rules

An important system when placing team-colored blocks during the build phase:

  • Applicable Blocks: Only applies to red/blue concrete and stained glass
  • Connection Required: Team-colored blocks must be connected to your team's beacon (flag), existing team blocks, or spawn points
  • Connection Definition: Must be adjacent in one of 6 directions (up, down, left, right, front, back) or within 3 blocks of flag/spawn
  • No Diagonal Connection: Diagonal connections are not considered valid
  • Other Blocks: Blocks purchased from the shop (stone, wood, dirt, etc.) can be placed freely without connection restrictions

Disconnection and Neutralization

  • Connection Check: When a team-colored block is destroyed, surrounding team blocks are automatically checked for connection
  • Isolated Block Neutralization: Team-colored blocks that lose their connection path to the beacon automatically turn white (quartz blocks)
  • Reusable: Neutralized blocks can be used as new connection points by either team
  • Other Blocks Unaffected: Regular blocks (stone, wood, etc.) are not subject to disconnection checks

üîÑ Respawn System

Multiple Spawn Points

Teams can have multiple spawn points. You'll respawn at a randomly selected location.

Respawn Delay

Base delay: 10 seconds (configurable per game)
Death penalty: +2 seconds per death (configurable)
Maximum delay: 20 seconds (configurable)

Spawn Protection

3 seconds of invincibility after respawn with glowing effect.
Protection is removed immediately if you attack or pick up a flag.

Phase Transitions

Players waiting to respawn during phase changes will respawn immediately with the correct game mode.

üìù Quick Tips

Communication is key: Use team chat to coordinate attacks and call out enemy positions.

Save currency: Pool team resources for important items like ender pearls or diamond gear.

Learn the map: Memorize flag locations and the fastest routes during build phase.

Protect carriers: Escort your flag carrier back to base for guaranteed points.

Use spawn protection: You have 3 seconds of invincibility after respawn - use it wisely! Protection is cancelled if you attack or pick up a flag.
