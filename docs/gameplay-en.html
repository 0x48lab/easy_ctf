How to Play

Master the basics of EasyCTF and become a champion!

üéØ Game Objective

The goal is simple: capture the enemy team's flag and bring it back to your base! The team with the most captures when time runs out wins.

Victory Condition: The team with the highest score at the end of combat phase wins. In case of a tie, the winner is determined by the number of colored blocks placed.

üö© Flag System

Flag Appearance

Flags are beacons with team-colored glass on top, placed on a 3x3 iron block base.

How to Capture

Walk within 1.5 blocks of the enemy flag to pick it up. The flag will appear above your head.

Scoring

Return to your base with the enemy flag while your own flag is at base to score 1 point.

When Killed

If you die while carrying the flag, it drops at your death location. It returns automatically after 15 seconds.

Flag Carrier Restrictions:
  • Cannot use ender pearls
  • Cannot use elytra
  • Cannot ride vehicles
  • Has glowing effect (visible through walls)

üõ°Ô∏è Build Phase Strategy

Defense Building

Create obstacles around your flag using the infinite team-colored blocks. Build walls, mazes, or towers to protect your flag.

Shop Usage

Right-click the emerald in your inventory near spawn to open the shop. Buy weapons, armor, and special items.

Team Coordination

Discuss strategies with your team. Decide who will attack and who will defend before combat begins.

üìù Quick Tips

Communication is key: Use team chat to coordinate attacks and call out enemy positions.

Save currency: Pool team resources for important items like ender pearls or diamond gear.

Learn the map: Memorize flag locations and the fastest routes during build phase.

Protect carriers: Escort your flag carrier back to base for guaranteed points.

Use spawn protection: You have 3 seconds of invincibility after respawn - use it wisely!
