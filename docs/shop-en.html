Shop System

Purchase items with team-shared currency to strengthen your forces

Overview

In EasyCTF's shop system, you can purchase items using the team-shared currency "G". Strengthen your equipment with currency earned during combat to gain an advantage in battle.

How to Use

Right-click the emerald
Within 15 blocks of spawn point

Team Sharing

All team members share the same currency pool

Discount System

Losing teams get up to 40% discount

Currency System

How to Earn Currency

Action Earned G Notes
Initial Currency 50G At the start of each game
Normal Kill 10G Defeat an enemy player
Flag Carrier Kill 20G Defeat an enemy carrying the flag
Kill Assist 5G Enemy defeated within 5 seconds of damage
Flag Carrier Assist 10G Assist on flag carrier
Flag Capture 30G Return flag to home base
Phase End Bonus 50G End of build/combat phase

Kill Streak Bonus

2 consecutive kills: +5G 3 consecutive kills: +10G 4 consecutive kills: +15G 5+ consecutive kills: +20G

Shop Strategy Guide

Early Game (0-50G)

  1. Secure basic food supplies
  2. Upgrade from stone sword to iron sword
  3. Purchase basic building blocks

Mid Game (50-150G)

  1. Strengthen defense with iron armor set
  2. Secure mobility with ender pearls
  3. Golden apples for emergency healing

Late Game (150G+)

  1. Transition to diamond/netherite equipment
  2. Enhance firepower with enchanted weapons
  3. Prepare for final battle with enchanted golden apples
